Welcome to AAAI-21!

This page describes how to use this website to find live events (at fixed times) plus pre-recorded talks you can watch anytime.

Schedule
Use the Schedule to find live sessions and set your timezone.
Plenary Sessions
{#
Plenary Sessions include Keynote Talks and the Business meeting (pre-recorded talks + live Q&A), plus Awards ceremonies.#} {# #} {# Pre-recorded videos are available under Plenary Sessions.#} {# #} {#
#}
Plenary Sessions will be held February 4-7, and will include the Conference Welcome and Awards, Keynote and Invited Talks, Invited Panels, and special community events, such as the Organizational Awards Ceremony. Some pre-recorded videos may be available for Plenary Sessions. Plenary sessions are scheduled for the following times:
  • 7:30 - 8:45 AM PST
  • 3:30 - 4:45 PM PST
  • 11:30 - 00:45 AM PST
    • Poster Sessions
    Conference-wide poster sessions will be held February 4-7, and will include posters from all programs listed below under Conference Papers (except EAAI-21). Poster sessions are scheduled for the following times:
  • 8:45 - 10:30 AM PST
  • 4:45 - 6:30 PM PST
  • 00:45 - 2:30 AM PST
    • Authors are encouraged to be available during two sessions each day.
    Sponsors/Exhibitors/Job Fair
    AAAI is grateful to all of its sponsors for their support of all aspects of the conference program. Please visit our sponsors and exhibitor booths during the following times, February 4-7:
  • 10:00 - 12:30 AM PST
  • 6:00 - 8:30 PM PST
  • 2:00 - 4:30 AM PST
    • Be sure to attend the AAAI/ACM SIGAI Job Fair on Saturday, February 6 during the following hours.
  • 10:30 - 12:30 AM PST
  • 6:30 - 8:30 PM PST
  • 2:30 - 4:30 AM PST
    • Tutorials and Workshops
    AAAI includes (25) tutorials on February 3 and (26) workshops on February 8-9, which are open to all registered attendees. Most will be live sessions, accessible via the posted Zoom link.
    Conference Papers
    Preliminary PDFs for all accepted papers for the following tracks are available via this site: AAAI Main Track, AAAI Special Track on AI for Social Impact, IAAI-21, EAAI-21, Senior Member Talks, Demonstrations, Student Abstract Program, Doctoral Consortium, and the Undergraduate Consortium.
    AAAI-21/IAAI-21 Conference Pre-recorded Talks
    Each AAAI-21 conference paper has a pre-recorded 20-minute talk, a pre-recorded 1-minute introductory talk (for a summary of the paper), and an associated poster session.
    Each IAAI-21 conference paper has a pre-recorded 20-minute talk, a pre-recorded 2-minute introductory talk (for a summary of the paper), and an associated poster session.
    Pre-recorded talks for the following programs are also available: Senior Member Talks, Demonstrations, Student Abstract Program, Doctoral Consortium, the Classic Paper Award Talk, and the AAAI/ACM SIGAI Dissertation Award Talk. Live Q&A video sessions (Zoom/Gather.Town) will be available via the assigned poster session times, as well as a linked RocketChat channel (for chatting anytime with the author).
    EAAI-21 Symposium
    The EAAI-21 will be conducted live via Zoom. All conference registrants are welcome.
    Doctoral and Undergraduate Consortia
    The Doctoral and Undergraduate Consortia will be conducted live via Zoom. All AAAI-21 registrants are welcome.
    Diversity Events
    AAAI features 11 Diversity and Inclusion events throughout the February 2-9 conference period. Most are open to conference registrants, and accessible via the posted Zoom link.
    Chat
    Chat with attendees using various RocketChat channels! When you access RocketChat for the first time, click on 'AAAI-2021 Login' and your AAAI2021 credentials will be automatically used to log you in to RocketChat.
    Help
    Look under Help for FAQs and technical support.
