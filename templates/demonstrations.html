Welcome to the AAAI-21 Demonstration Program!

The AAAI-21 Demonstrations Program is intended to foster discussion and exchange of ideas among researchers and practitioners from academia and industry by presenting software and hardware systems and research prototypes of such systems, including their capabilities and workings. All accepted demos are scheduled for presentation February 4-7. Accepted demonstrations also have a short paper available for viewing throughout the conference.

Demo presentations will fall in the conference-wide poster/demo session time slots:

8:45 - 10:30 AM PST
4:45 - 6:30 PM PST
12:45 - 2:30 AM PST

Each demo poster will be available during all three time segments on the day it is scheduled. We have encouraged presenters to be present in at least two of the three presentation windows.

Kartik Talamadupula and Cynthia Matuszek

Demonstrations Program Cochairs
