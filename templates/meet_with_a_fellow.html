Welcome to the Meet with a AAAI Fellow Program

First held in 2006, the AAAI Fellow/Student program provides an opportunity for a small number of students to chat with a AAAI Fellow in an informal setting during the conference. This year the program will be held via Gather.town. Sign-up sheets will be available in the room, and a volunteer will assist you in locating your chosen Fellow at the designated time.

Fellows will generally be available for one hour in one of the following time periods.

Feburary 4 - 6
------------------
12:00 - 14:00 PST
20:00 - 22:00 PST
04:00 - 06:00 PST (Feburary 5-7)
